Marietta Museum of Art & Whimsy

Recommended donation of $5-$10

Our Mission is to Operate an Extraordinary Art and Whimsical Museum for the public by collecting, preserving and exhibiting works of Artistic and Whimsical importance to Educate our visitors about our culture and history. We have created an environment to Inspire, Restore and Lift our Spirits and Heart.

Hours: Thurs-Sat 1pm - 4pm

St. Armands Circle

Free

This is a great place to go walk around and shop in your free time. It is walking distance to the beach and has a variety of shops for clothing, swimsuits, food, and especially sweets!

Hours: Sun-Sat (most shops close around 8-9)

Bayfront Park

Free

Pets are allowed outside the Steigerwaldt-Jockey Children’s Fountain area. Popular location to jog, enjoy the sunset, walk your leashed pet or have a picnic at the playground. There are also two bayfront restaurants. Owned and operated by the City of Sarasota Parks & Recreation District.

Hours: Sun-Sat 7am - 11pm

Sarasota Farmer's Market

Free

Our mission is to create a social opportunity for residents and visitors to gather and interact, to bring foot traffic to downtown Sarasota supporting the economic viability of the entire downtown area, and to provide a venue for other non-profit organizations benefiting our community.

Hours: Sat 7am - 1pm
