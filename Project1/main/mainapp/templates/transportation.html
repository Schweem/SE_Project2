Breeze Transit

Free with NCF ID

The dropoff spot is located across from the airport right when you exit the Sudakoff parking lot. Please see the website for certain routes to your requested drop off location.

Hours: Mon-Sat 5am - 11pm, Sun 6am - 10pm

Bus To TPA Airport

Around $20

The departure spot is at Sarasota Cattlemen Transfer Center and drops off at Tampa Bus Station or University of South Florida. This will mean you will need to find additional transportation to and from the bus stops, but they are only around a 15-20 min ride.

Hours: *Departure times vary.

Sarasota Bay Runner

Free with NCF ID

The route is included above. You can ride your bike or park downtown and then take the trolley to the beach or somewhere on St. Armand's Circle.

Hours: Sun-Thurs 7am - 10pm, Fri-Sat 7am - 11pm
